1.5x Augmentation canon in 4 voices

In renaissance music there are some examples of augmentation canons where all notes values are multiplied by a factor of 1.5. Such as the Agnus Dei II from Des Prez' first Missa L'homme armé or some of the canons in the Missa Prolationum by Ockeghem. I have however not found these pieces in post-renaissance tonal music, so I tried my hand at one:

https://musescore.com/user/6168221/scores/5606505

If anybody knows of more examples, I'd love to hear them. Discussion on doing this more convincingly is especially welcome, I love the sound of it.
